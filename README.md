## 📂 What is PARA + Zettelkasten?

**PARA** helps divide your notes into four groups:

- **Projects:** Active tasks and goals.
- **Areas:** Long-term responsibilities.
- **Resources:** Topics or references you use.
- **Archives:** Inactive or old notes.

**Zettelkasten** is a note-taking method that uses unique IDs and links to connect ideas. It helps your notes grow into a network of knowledge.

zettelvault combines these methods automatically so you can focus on writing rather than organizing.
